Research Interests

My research focuses on the interconnection and integration of large flexible loads, with a particular emphasis on Data Centers. I investigate their potential market impacts and conduct rigorous congestion analyses to facilitate their seamless integration into the power grid. Ultimately, I aim to develop data-driven strategies that ensure grid reliability and economic efficiency amidst the rapid growth of energy-intensive assets.
